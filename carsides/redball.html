Red Ball

Red Ball was considered by many to be the most notorious producer of printed sides in HO scale, offering hundreds of different cars over their decades-long production run. The bulk of their production consisted of dozens upon dozens of different wood reefers, far more than ever offered by any other manufacturer, including many obscure roadnames unique to their catalog.
